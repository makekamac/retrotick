Adding a widget

Touch and hold the Home Screen (or Lock Screen), tap + / Edit, search for FlipClock, and add the size you like.

24-hour time & seconds

Open FlipClock and use the in-app settings to toggle 24-hour time and to show or hide seconds. Widgets update to match.

Themes & restoring purchases

Open the theme gallery to preview any theme. Premium themes are one-time purchases. If you've bought a theme and it appears locked — for example on a new device — tap Restore Purchases in the theme gallery. Your unlocks are tied to your Apple ID.

Live Activity & Dynamic Island

Start the Live Activity from the in-app control to keep FlipClock on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island. It mirrors your current time and your chosen font, seconds, and glass settings.
