Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Retrotick does not collect any personal data. It has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking. There is nothing to opt out of, because nothing is collected.

Information we collect

None. Retrotick does not gather, store, or transmit any personal or usage data off your device.

Your settings

Your preferences — the selected theme, 12/24-hour time, seconds, glass backdrop, digit font, and which themes you've unlocked — are stored locally on your device (and shared with the Retrotick widgets on the same device). They never leave your device and are never sent to us or anyone else.

Purchases

Premium themes are one-time purchases handled entirely by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. Retrotick never sees or stores your payment information. Apple's handling of a purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

Retrotick is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
