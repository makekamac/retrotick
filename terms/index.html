You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your device, or in the App Store by tapping your account picture and choosing Subscriptions. One-time theme purchases

Premium themes can also be bought individually as one-time (non-consumable) purchases. A purchased theme stays unlocked permanently for your Apple Account and doesn't require a subscription. If a purchased theme ever shows as locked — for example on a new device — tap Restore Purchases in the theme gallery to bring your unlocks back.
